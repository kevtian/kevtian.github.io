Kevin Ti-and?

Hello! I'm a junior at Duke University, studying Computer Science with a focus on Psychology and Human Centered Design.

On campus, most of my free time doing design with the HackDuke Design Team, dancing traditional and modern style dance with Duke Chinese Dance, and planning social events with LD SLG House Council.

I love following trends in consumer technology, so something I often think about is how we manage to keep up with technology that is constantly evolving. I write thoughts down in my notebook and occassionally, I publish them on Medium. My first and latest article is on the Spotify playlist UX, take a look here.

Most recently, my browser tabs, Figma and InVision screens, and notebook have housed: a redesign of a mobile app onboarding sequence, a street map visual redesign for greater clarity, and a refined playlist user experience design for everyday listeners like myself and my friends.
