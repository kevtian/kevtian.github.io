about me



I'm currently a sophomore at Duke University pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science. My focus in and out of the classroom is on design. I'm currently reading: The Design of Everyday Things by Don Norman.



When taking breaks from staring at a computer screen, I love exploring all that nature has to offer. To the left is a picture of me from this past summer, standing at the summit of Chile's most active volcano, Villarrica.



In addition to learning about new tech and traveling the world, one of my favorite hobbies is photography.
