- core design principles

The most beautiful design is

honest.

✨

It empower users and make their lives easier, and it doesn't mislead or decieve.

minimal.

✨

It captures all of the essentials and hides the rest. A simple yet delightful experience.

thorough.

✨

It is designed with the user—not just for the user—in a process that can’t be rushed.

My previous design experiences reflect my passion for empowering underserved users and highlight my ability to design with these guiding principles even when faced with unique social & tech constraints.
