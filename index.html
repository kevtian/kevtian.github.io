- core design principles

The most beautiful design is

honest.

Empower people and makes their lives easier, without misleading or deceiving.

minimal.

Capture the essentials—and hide the rest.

thorough.

Design and refine solutions with the user to push past expectations.

Each of my previous product design experiences reflect my passion for empowering underserved users and highlight my ability to design with these guiding principles even when faced with unique constraints.
